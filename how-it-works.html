How it Works

Trusted.com has established itself as the new industry standard for product verification and authentication services. Trusted.com software-as-a-service (SaaS) solution provides secure and transferrable electronic titles (eTitles) for luxury products. A Trusted.com eTitle is an electronic title that establishes rightful ownership of product. eTitles provide a detailed history of ownership for a product beginning with the origination of a title at the time of the product is manufactured and throughout its distribution of the product and subsequent sales.

The system tracks all changes in ownership from the time the product is manufactured to the time it is distributed through a authorized supply chain, and ultimately to the point the product is purchased by a consumer and held or resold. These transferrable eTitles offer a new standard for verifying authenticity and provides numerous benefits, including: increased brand protection, the ability to challenge unauthorized sellers as well as enhanced consumer protection to prevent the purchase of luxury goods which are fraudulent or misrepresented in the marketplace.
