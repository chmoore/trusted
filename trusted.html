How It Works

Trusted.com has established itself as the new industry standard for product verification and authentication services. Trusted.com software-as-a-service (SaaS) solution provides secure and transferrable electronic titles (eTitles) for luxury products. The Trusted eTitles are created at the time a product is manufactured.

The system then tracks all changes in ownership from the time the product is manufactured through the distribution of the product through authorized supply chain, and ultimately to the point the product is purchased by a consumer and held or resold. These transferrable eTitles offer a new standard for verifying authenticity and provides numerous benefits, including: increased brand protection, the ability to challenge unauthorized sellers as well as enhanced consumer protection to prevent the purchase of luxury goods which are fraudulent or misrepresented in the marketplace.
