How It Works

Trusted.com has established itself as the new industry standard for product verification and authentication services. Trusted.com software-as-a-service (SaaS) solution provides secure and transferrable electronic titles (eTitles) for luxury products. The Trusted eTitles are created at the time a product is manufactured.

These transferrable eTitles offer a new standard for verifying authenticity and provides numerous benefits, including: increased brand protection, the ability to challenge unauthorized sellers as well as enhanced consumer protection to prevent the purchase of luxury goods which are fraudulent or misrepresented in the marketplace.
