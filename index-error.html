How It Works

Trusted.com has established itself as the new industry standard for product verification and authentication services. Trusted.com software-as-a-service (SaaS) solution provides secure and transferrable electronic titles (eTitles) for luxury products. The Trusted eTitles are created at the time a product is manufactured.
